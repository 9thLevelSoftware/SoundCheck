## Decisions Made
- BadgeEvaluators intentionally NOT filtered -- the user attended the show and that credit should not be revoked when a photo is flagged; only user-facing content feeds/stats are filtered
- ModerationService invalidates the content author's own feed cache (in addition to followers) since the author may also see their content in event feeds
- Comment hiding traces back to the parent checkin to resolve the correct user_id and event_id for cache invalidation
- Cache invalidation uses fire-and-forget .catch(logError) pattern matching CheckinCreatorService convention
